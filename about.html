This is Thea's About!

"Let your attitude be your voice"

Althea Vidal Rabadon is a 3rd year BS Information Technology student at Ilocos Sur Polytechnic State college. A once dreamer that's still dreaming. Her father died when she was three and her mother needs to work in another city for them to have enough support. Since then, she started living at her grandparents house, she literally grew up with her grandparents. Her dream course was Doctor of Veterinary Medicine, but because of financial problems and self-doubt, she pursued Information Technology.

She's not good at everything but she can always try, because someone says, trying is better than doing nothing. She can go beyond her capabilities and always willing to learn. She doesn't have a good past but she's working really hard to have a better present and make her future self proud. She survive the days she thought she couldn't, a tough woman who will soon create a name out there for herself.
